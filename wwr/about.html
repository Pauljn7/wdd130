About Us

Our History

Founded in 1998, White Water Rafting Adventures began with a single goal—to help people experience the thrill of river rafting while building respect for nature. What started as a small family business on the Snake River has grown into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region.

Over the years, we’ve guided thousands of adventurers safely down rivers across the country. Our commitment to safety, fun, and environmental stewardship remains the foundation of all we do.

Adventure Awaits You!

Join us for an unforgettable experience surrounded by breathtaking scenery and the rush of the rapids. Whether you’re a first-time rafter or a seasoned pro, we have the perfect trip for you!
