About River Rush Adventures

Experience the power and beauty of Nigeria’s rivers with our professional rafting team. Your adventure begins here!

Our Story

River Rush Adventures started back in 2012 along the Ogbese River in Ondo State, Nigeria. It began with a small group of friends who loved exploring rivers and wanted to share that excitement with others. What started as weekend trips soon grew into a full rafting tour company serving families, students, and adventure lovers from all over West Africa.

We believe that adventure brings people together. Every trip is designed to help our guests connect with nature, build confidence, and experience the thrill of the rapids safely.

Adventure Awaits!

Whether you are rafting the strong currents of the Niger River or gliding through the calm bends of Cross River, every moment with us is an experience you’ll never forget. Our guides are trained professionals who make sure safety and fun come first.
